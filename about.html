About MediCare Plus Hospital

Trusted healthcare for over 15 years in Nandyal

Who We Are

A legacy of care, compassion and excellence

MediCare Plus Hospital was founded in 2010 with a simple mission — to provide affordable, world-class healthcare to every patient in Nandyal and surrounding areas. With over 150+ doctors and 800+ staff members, we serve thousands of patients every day.

We believe every person deserves the best medical care regardless of their background. Our hospital is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by the most experienced medical professionals in Andhra Pradesh.

Our Values: Integrity, compassion, excellence, and respect for every patient who walks through our doors.
